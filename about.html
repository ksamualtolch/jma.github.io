JMA Marketing

John Manson & Associates represents a number of fine manufacturers in both the Hospitality and Contract Furnishings Industries. We were incorporated in February 2004, representing product lines in Illinois, Wisconsin and Northwest Indiana. Our 2500 square foot showroom in Chicago was opened in December 2004. We have had some of Chicago's and the Nation's finest restaurants and hotels select dinnerware, flatware, furniture and accessories from among our offerings on display.

Our goal has always been to represent the finest manufacturers available. Companies which have both unique products and a reputation for outstanding quality are of utmost importance to us, as well as the sales representatives and specifiers who sell our product lines. We work closely with the ultimate end-users of our product lines to ensure that they receive the very best service, quality, and follow through on their orders. We pride ourselves on all aspects of customer service, realizing that our own success depends on the level of service which we provide. We maintain a very high sense of personal values with a heavy focus on honesty, integrity and decency.
